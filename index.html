Quick Password Security Tips

  • Always use at least 12 characters for your passwords ‚Äî longer is better.
  • Include a mix of lowercase letters, uppercase letters, numbers, and special symbols.
  • Avoid using easily guessable information like birthdays, names, or simple sequences (e.g., "123456").
  • Use a different password for every important account to minimize risks.
  • Consider using a password manager to securely store your passwords.
